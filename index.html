How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

Get up and running in under a minute.

Register, add payment, get your API key

Create an account with your email, add your payment method, and set your target URL. Your API key is generated instantly.

curl -X POST https://webhooker-production.up.railway.app/register \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"email":"you@example.com","target_url":"https://your-webhook.com"}'

💡 Tip: Use webhook.site as your target URL to see payloads in real-time.

Send a webhook to our endpoint

Point your source application at our /convert endpoint with your API key.

curl -X POST https://webhooker-production.up.railway.app/convert \
  -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"hello":"world"}'

We transform & forward — you pay per conversion

That's the whole flow. We handle conversion, delivery, and retries. $0.01/conversion for your first 30 days, then $0.01–$0.06 per conversion based on volume and complexity.
